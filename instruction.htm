Instructions



The participating candidates have to report to the venue by 5:00 P.M. Late entries will not be entertained.
The time limit is 3 minutes. A warning bell will be given at the end of 2 minutes and a final bell will be given at the end of 3 minutes. Penalty points (-5) after time & thereon (-5) will be deducted for every exceeding 10 seconds.
The team should have at least three members and maximum seven members.
No accompanying instruments should be used.
The team securing first place will get a Rolling shield. Prizes will be awarded to the teams securing first three places, only if the total participating team is 15 or more than 15 else first two places will be awarded.
The decision of the judges will be final and binding under all circumstances.
ALL THE BEST!
